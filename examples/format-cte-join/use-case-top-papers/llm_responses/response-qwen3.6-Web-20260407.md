## ⚠️ Important Limitations

2. **Disciplinary differences**: Physics/math papers are more likely to appear on arXiv; CS papers may be in conference proceedings with delayed indexing.
3. **Name ambiguity**: Common names (e.g., "Charles Bennett") require careful disambiguation across fields.
4. **Non-traditional outputs**: Some laureates publish policy reports, lectures, or commentary pieces not counted as "papers" in academic databases.
